These web pages hopefully provide some information that may help your stay at the flat.

Utility cut-offs

  • Gas - the main cut-off valve is on the gas pipe by the gas meter, in the cupboard by the kitchen window.
  • Electricity - the main switch is on the consumer unit, in the cupboard by the kitchen window.
  • Water - the riser stop cock is at the back of the cupboard under the oven.

Detectors and Alarms

It is the occupants' responsibility, and in their best interest, to notice flat batteries in the detectors - generally it's very easy to do:
  • smoke detectors - each has a push button battery check: if they beep when pushed then they're working!
  • carbon monoxide alarm - starts to beep and show 'lb' when the battery is low.
  • security alarm - the number pad has a low battery warning light: if lit, tell the landlord.
